Author's Kit - ACM Publication

General

  • Please prepare your paper according to the SIMUTools 2009 Author's Kit.
  • Please choose the ACM SIG Proceedings Template which includes the ACM copyright and document/conference identification at the bottom of the left column. This box must remain in this location on your paper.

Workshop Guidelines

  • If you are using LaTeX to prepare your paper, please fill in the document/conference identification with the following values (updated 2009-01-19):
    \conferenceinfo{OMNeT++ 2009,}{Rome, Italy.}
    \CopyrightYear{2009}
    \crdata{978-963-9799-45-5}
    \copyrightetc={Copyright \the\copyrtyr\ ICST, ISBN \the\acmcopyr}
    If you use a different template, please adapt it according to the copyright block sample.
  • Regular papers are allowed up to 8 pages, short papers up to 4 pages, including all figures, tables and references.
