Submission Guidelines

The instructions for final paper submission given on the Simutools website also apply for the OMNeT++ workshop (see their Author's Kit and Submission guidelines). In short: One of the authors has to register at the full rate for the workshop. After that, you will receive a login for CMC, where you can upload your final paper. You will also have to sign a copyright form.
